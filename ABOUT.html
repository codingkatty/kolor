The Birth of Kolor

Kolor, a Japanese fashion brand, was founded in 2004 by Junichi Abe. With a rich background in pattern making and a keen eye for innovative design, Abe set out to create a brand that would redefine contemporary fashion.

Junichi Abe: The Visionary Designer

Junichi Abe, the creative force behind Kolor, honed his skills at Comme des Garçons and Junya Watanabe. His expertise in pattern making and fabric manipulation has become the cornerstone of Kolor's unique aesthetic.

Kolor's Design Philosophy

Kolor is renowned for its sophisticated approach to casual wear. The brand seamlessly blends high-end tailoring with sportswear elements, creating a distinctive style that appeals to fashion-forward individuals worldwide.

Global Recognition and Expansion

Since its inception, Kolor has gained international acclaim for its innovative designs and quality craftsmanship. The brand has expanded its presence globally, showcasing collections at Paris Fashion Week and establishing a loyal following among fashion enthusiasts.

Why Choose Us?
